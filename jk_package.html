Kashmir

Detail Itinerary
  • Day 1 :

    Arrive Delhi

    Traditional welcome by our executive on your arrival at Delhi’s airport followed by transfer to the hotel.

    Overnight at hotel in Delhi.

  • Day 02:

    Delhi

    Breakfast at the hotel.

    Today morning explore and experience the capital of Muslim India between the 12th and 20th centuries, the Old Delhi. We shall stop at the Red Fort for a photo shoot and then take a Rickshaw ride through the narrow streets of the Old Delhi up to the nearby Jama Masjid Mosque, the largest in India, has a courtyard that can hold 25,000 worshippers. The pay a poignant visit to Rajghat, the site of Mahatma Gandhi’s cremation and a memorial to this humble man who so changed the world.

    In the afternoon we explore India’s bustling capital city the New Delhi visiting the Vijay Chowk, “Victory Square”, stopover at the Presidential Palace with its intriguing combination of classical and Indian design elements and the India Gate memorializing Indian soldiers who died in World War I. Then travel far back in time at Humayun’s Tomb, a UNESCO World Heritage Site dating from 1570 AD and the first garden-tomb on the Indian sub-continent and lastly visit the Qutab Minar, the tallest brick minaret in the world.

    Overnight at hotel in Delhi.

  • Day 03:

    Delhi – Srinagar (By Flight)

    Breakfast at the hotel.

    In time transfer to the airport to connect flight to Srinagar. At Srinagar airport you will be assisted for transfer to your hotel.

    Evening enjoy a Shikara ride on Dal Lake.

    Overnight at hotel Srinagar.

  • Day 04:

    Srinagar – Sonmarg – Srinagar (By Surface: 90 kms / 2½ hrs onway)

    Breakfast at the hotel.

    Depart for full day excursion to Sonmarg. Enjoy beautiful snow capped peaks and glaciers.

    Return to Srinagar in the late afternoon. Evening is free for leisure activities.

    Overnight at hotel in Srinagar.

  • Day 05:

    Srinagar – Pahalgam (By Surface: 110 kms / 4 hrs)

    Breakfast at the hotel.

    Start your drive to Pahalgam, en-route visit Avantipura Ruins. On arrival at Pahalgam, check In to your hotel.

    Balance of the day free at leisure.

    Overnight at hotel in Pahalgam.

  • Day 06:

    Phalagham

    Today after breakfast, depart for excursion trip to Aru, Chandanwadi, Betab Valley.

    Evening is free for leisure activities.

    Overnight at hotel in Pahalgam.

    Note: For sightseeing around Pahalagam, you need to hire a local vehicle on direct payment basis as local taxi union does not allow outside vehicle to do sightseeing. Our driver will help you in booking local taxi.

  • Day 07:

    Phalagham – Gulmarg (By Surface: 140 kms / 4-5 hrs)

    After breakfast today you check out and start drive to Gulmarg. On reaching Gulmarg, check into your hotel.

    In the afternoon proceed for Gondola ride up to first phase.

    Evening is free for leisure.

    Overnight at hotel in Gulmarg.

    Note: Again in Gulmarg too, no outside vehicle is allowed. You need to take local vehicle / Ponies on direct payment basis as local taxi union does not allow outside vehicle to do sightseeing. Our driver will help you in booking local taxi / Pony.

  • Day 08:

    Gulmarg – Srinagar (By Surface: 90 kms / 2-3 hrs)

    Breakfast at the hotel and check out to start your drive back to Srinagar.

    Afternoon visit the Nishat, Shalimar and Chashma Shahi the three famous Mughal gardens which are situated around the Dal Lake. These Mughal gardens are beautifully laid out with fountains and cascading streams and terraced lawns. These Mughal Gardens were the Mughal Emperors' concept of paradise and are today very popular places for picnic and excursion.

    Evening free for shopping.

    Overnight at hotel in Srinagar.

  • Day 09:

    Srinagar – Delhi (By Flight)

    Breakfast at the hotel. In time transfer to the airport to connect flight back to Delhi.

    On arrival in Delhi you will be assisted for transfer to your hotel.

    Overnight at hotel in Delhi.

  • Day 10:

    Delhi Depart

    In time transfer to the international airport to connect flight back home.
